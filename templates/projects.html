HackIllinois App

HackIllinois is a student run hackathon at the University of Illinois. As a member of the core Systems Team, I collaboratively developed the Android app and contibuted to the backend of the main website.

Engineering Open House App

Engineering Open House (EOH) is an annual student event that welcomes over 15,000 visitors from around the world to explore student engineering projects.

This year I threw out the old mobile app and designed a new app from scratch. I developed the Android app myself and collaborated with another iOS developer to produce a uniform design and user experience.

EWS Labs App

As MP deadlines approach, University computer labs often fill to capacity as students scramble to debug and submit their code. In order to mitigate this problem, I created an Android app that allows users to monitor the availability of computers in every lab on campus.

Plumbing Paradox

Plumbing Paradox was my first venture into computer programming. It is a 2D puzzle game on Android that uses the libGDX game library.
